How it works
  • Each offer card gets a trade section below the shipping info, separated by a divider
  • Shows the card art thumbnail, "Or trade your" label, card name + details
  • "Propose trade" button (outlined, not filled — secondary action to buying)
  • The wanted card is generated per-offer from the seller's mock trade preferences
  • Tapping "Propose trade" navigates to the trade screen to build the trade
