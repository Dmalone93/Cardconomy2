Free Shipping Upsell: When should it appear?

After a user adds a card to cart from a seller with multiple listings, how do we nudge them?

A

Toast / Snackbar

A small banner slides up from the bottom right after "Added to cart" — e.g. "Add £12 more from VaultCards for free shipping". Tapping it goes to the seller's profile. Auto-dismisses after a few seconds. Lightweight, non-blocking.

B

Bottom Sheet

A slide-up sheet appears showing: the item you just added, the free shipping threshold, and 2-3 suggested cards from the same seller. User can add more or dismiss. More prominent, higher conversion but more interruptive.

C

Inline on Product Page

After adding to cart, the "Add to cart" button area transforms to show the upsell message inline — "✓ Added · £12 more from this seller = free shipping" with a link to the seller profile. No popup, stays in context.

My recommendation: B (Bottom Sheet)

It gives enough room to show the threshold AND suggest specific cards from the seller — which directly drives the bundling behavior you want. The existing app already has a Sheet component we can reuse.
