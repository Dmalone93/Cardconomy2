Design decisions
  • Seller name on offer cards becomes a tappable blue link → opens their storefront
  • "View all X listings" link added below each offer card for discoverability
  • Storefront screen — branded header, stats, bio, tabs (Listings/Reviews/Policies). Stubbed data with sensible defaults until real seller data exists
  • Upsell toast — only shows when: seller has other listings AND user hasn't hit free shipping threshold. Tapping "Browse →" navigates to seller storefront. Auto-dismisses after 4s
  • Free shipping threshold — added to seller data model (default £50, configurable per seller)
