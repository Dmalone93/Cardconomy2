Objection Handling

Common questions

"This is just another marketplace"

It is the only one with local shop infrastructure. Every other marketplace is shipping-only. We connect the trades that happen in person — something no other platform does.

"Why would a shop use this?"

Free deal flow. Sellers scan before they walk in. The shop reviews from the counter and never has to manually sort a stack again. Free upgrade to their intake process.

"How do you compete with eBay?"

We do not compete head-on. We win on specialisation — card-specific scanning, pricing, grading, and authentication that eBay will never build, plus the local network they cannot replicate.

"Is this just a prototype?"

Fully interactive with working navigation, state persistence, and realistic data. Every flow walks through end to end. We are demonstrating the product vision, not static mockups.
